Weather Mate (M8)


Permissions

RECEIVE BOOT COMPLETED - to start background updating after phone reboots
READ/WRITE EXTERNAL STORAGE - to make screenshots (share function)
INTERNET - internet permission (get data from the internet)
ACCESS NETWORK STATE - check if internet is accessible
LOCATION - locate device (get your coordinates to load forecast for your current location)
GET ACCOUNTS - this is what you get as "Contacts" permission warning. This permission shows as Contacts because it's in the Contacts group of permissions (it won't let the app to read your contacts list). It lets the app get the list of registered on your device accounts emails to check if your account is in ads-free list.
