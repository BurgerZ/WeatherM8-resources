Not all weather providers used in this application support all weather data.

Limitations

  • Yr.no

    • No precipitation probability for current conditions

    • No rain/snow precipitation differentiation

  • Caiyun (Colorful Clouds)

    • No precipitation intensity data

    • No rain/snow precipitation differentiation

    • No dew point temperature

    • No visibility data

  • Foreca

    • No precipitation probability for current conditions

    • No rain/snow precipitation differentiation

  • The Weather Channel

    • No precipitation probability for current conditions

  • Weather Underground

    • No precipitation probability for current conditions
